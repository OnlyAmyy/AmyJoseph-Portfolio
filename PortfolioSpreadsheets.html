UNIVERSITY BUDGETING SHEET


Before I started university, I decided to make myself a spreadsheet which I could use to figure out a weekly allowance from my student loan and how much I’d have leftover from each week that I didn’t spend the weekly allowance. The spreadsheet takes each semester’s loan amount and divides it by how many weeks are in that semester to work out a weekly allowance and if you spend over the weekly allowance then the cell’s contents will turn red. A total and an average is also calculated at the end of each semester, which has been useful for me when comparing last year’s expenditure to this years.
